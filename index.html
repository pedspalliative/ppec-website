What makes PPEC different

An interprofessional lens on both the clinical and the human

Interprofessional

Advanced nursing practice and pediatric psychology on the same team. The clinical and the human, taught together.

Tailored

A proposal built in collaboration with you, scoped to your team, your goals, and the realities you face.

Interactive

Practice, not lecture. Sessions include role-play, real-world scenarios, small-group discussion, and guided feedback.

Lasting

Mentorship and follow-up are built in, because skill stays only when there's somewhere to take it.
